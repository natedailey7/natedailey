Mappy

About

Since the beginning of my sophomore year of college, I've been a research assistant for Professor Joseph Osborn, in a team of students to work on an application called Mappy. To sum it up, Mappy makes maps of NES games using a variety of sophisticed techniques.

I co-authored an academic paper on Mappy for AIIDE, a confenerence on AI and interactive digital entertainment. This research paper won the award of best paper at the confenerence.
