About Mappy

Since the beginning of my sophomore year of college (August 2020), I've been a research assistant for Professor Joseph Osborn in a team of students to work on an application called Mappy. To sum it up, Mappy makes maps of NES games using a variety of sophisticed techniques. I specifically worked on Avatar Detection and Sprite Blobbing features of the program.

In October, we submitted a paper on Mappy for AIIDE (conference on AI and Interactive Digital Entertainment). This research paper won the award of best paper at the conference.
